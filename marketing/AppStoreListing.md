## Description (4000 chars)

```
Most apps measure what you did. Pari measures the gap between what you said you'd do and what actually happened.

Lock in a prediction with a confidence percentage. When it resolves, Pari scores you using proper scoring rules — Brier score, calibration curves, the same math used by superforecasters at Good Judgment and Metaculus.

Over time, you stop being surprised by yourself.

—— THE HONESTY ENGINE ——

• Resolve-before-reveal: Pari hides your original confidence at resolution. You answer first. No anchoring on your past bet.
• Wall of Shame: your worst high-confidence misses, surfaced. Read these monthly.
• "I'm fudging" button: a face-saving way out. People are more honest when given one.
• Locked criteria: write what counts as "yes" up front; you can't move the goalpost.

—— THE SCORE ——

• Brier score, log score, calibration curves
• Benchmarks against Metaculus (0.111), Manifold (0.168), Tetlock-style superforecasters (~0.08)
• By category, time horizon, mood, and day of week
• Pattern detector: "you're 14 points more overconfident in the morning"

—— PARI FOR AI ——

The only personal calibration app that scores LLMs alongside you. Paste an answer from GPT, Claude, or Gemini, set your confidence in it, and watch both you and the model get scored on resolution. Pari aggregates a live hallucination calibration index per model.

—— BUILT FOR iOS ——

• Apple Watch app + Smart Stack widget
• App Intents for Siri ("Hey Siri, lock in a prediction in Pari at 70%")
• Live Activities for due predictions
• Spotlight indexing
• Background recompute via BGAppRefreshTask
• Apple Foundation Models for on-device prompts
• Privacy: local-first, no account, no tracking, optional iCloud sync

—— NO ——

No streaks. No badges. No coach. No virtual pet. No leaderboard you can't opt out of. No celebration on a correct prediction. The score is the score.

—— PRICING ——

• Free forever — unlimited predictions, full scoring, calibration curve, training mode
• Pro one-time: export, themes, annual report PDF, question packs
• Cloud yearly: everything in Pro plus iCloud sync and AI prompts

—— FROM ELSEWHERE? ——

Import from Fatebook, PredictionBook, or any spreadsheet with a `claim` column.

Pari is the calibration company. Wherever someone is making a confident claim about an uncertain future — themselves, a team, an AI, a public market — Pari is the layer that scores them.
```
