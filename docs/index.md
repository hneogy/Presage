# Présage

**Personal calibrated forecasting for iOS.**

Most people think they're right 90% of the time. They're actually right about 70%. The difference is overconfidence — and you can't fix it without measuring it.

Présage is a private, on-device tool for measuring how well-calibrated your predictions about your own life actually are. You predict things — what you'll get done, how a meeting will go, whether the relationship will work — with a confidence percentage and a resolution date. When the date comes, you answer honestly. Présage scores you using proper scoring rules and shows you the gap between what you said and what happened.

This is not a habit tracker, journal, or prediction market. It's a solo, honest mirror. No streaks, no badges, no gamification. The score is the score.

---

## The honesty mechanic

When a prediction resolves, Présage hides your original confidence and asks you Yes / No / Ambiguous first. Only after you answer does it reveal what you said. This deliberate friction prevents the most common form of self-deception: anchoring on your past confidence and finding ways to call yourself "basically right."

## What you get

- **Brier score and calibration curve** — the gold standards of forecasting research
- **Breakdowns** by category, time horizon, and mood at prediction time
- **Year-in-pixels heatmap** of your year of forecasts
- **Wall of shame** — your most overconfident misses
- **Calibration training** with pre-resolved questions for fast feedback
- **On-device LLM coach** (iOS 18+) for criteria sharpening and weekly retrospectives
- **Cognitive bias encyclopedia** — named biases Présage watches for in your patterns
- **Life Forecast** — 5-year vision in 5 domains, each milestone resolvable
- **Recurring templates**, **annual calibration report PDF**, **question packs**
- **AI scoring** — pose the same question to GPT or Claude alongside your own
- **Twin predictions**, **Présage for Teams**
- **CSV import**, **Markdown / Obsidian export**
- **Widgets**, **Lock Screen widgets**, **Live Activities**, **watch app**, **Siri shortcuts**

## Private by default

Présage collects nothing. Every prediction lives on your device. No analytics SDKs. No telemetry. No crash reporting. The privacy manifest declares zero tracking and zero collected data types — producing the strongest "Data Not Collected" privacy nutrition label on the App Store.

iCloud sync is optional and off by default. HealthKit mood pre-fill is optional. Bring-your-own-key AI features call providers directly from your device; we never proxy or log a single token.

## The math

Brier score, log score, and calibration bucketing follow standard formulations from forecasting research — Tetlock's Good Judgment work, Roulston & Smith's reliability diagrams, and the original Brier (1950) paper. The Knowledge Center inside the app explains every score, every privacy decision, every gesture, and every feature in plain language.
